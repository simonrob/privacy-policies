APPropriate privacy policy

This privacy policy has been compiled to better serve those who are concerned with how their Personally Identifiable Information is being used. Personally Identifiable Information is information that can be used on its own or with other information to identify, contact, or locate a single person, or to identify an individual in context. Please read our privacy policy carefully to get a clear understanding of how we collect, use, protect or otherwise handle your Personally Identifiable Information.

What personal information do we collect?

APPropriate does not collect or share any personal information.

Any information you choose to save to your accompanying hardware module using APPropriate (e.g., photos, audio recordings, text messages) is stored only on your that device. When you access your media from a guest device it is temporarily loaded on to that device in order to be able to display it to you. This usage is temporary, and the data is deleted whenever you log out of a guest device.

When do we collect information?

No information is collected by APPropriate.

How do we use your information?

No information is collected by APPropriate.

How do we protect your information?

No information is collected by APPropriate.

Third-party disclosure

APPropriate does not sell, trade or otherwise transfer your information.

Third-party products or services

APPropriate does not include or offer third-party products or services.
