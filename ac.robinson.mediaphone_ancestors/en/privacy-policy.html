Orígenes Privacy policy

This privacy policy has been compiled to better serve those who are concerned with how their Personally Identifiable Information is being used. Personally Identifiable Information is information that can be used on its own or with other information to identify, contact, or locate a single person, or to identify an individual in context. Please read our privacy policy carefully to get a clear understanding of how we collect, use, protect or otherwise handle your Personally Identifiable Information.

What personal information do we collect?

Orígenes does not collect or share any personal information.

Any information you choose to capture using Orígenes (e.g., photos, audio recordings, text) is stored only on your own device. Your narratives and media will not be shared or transmitted beyond your own device unless you choose to do so using another app or service, at which point other privacy policies may apply.

If you choose to use the community repository feature, Orígenes accesses the internet to retrieve images and descriptions based on your requests. The Orígenes community repository may collect usage data in the form of server access logs. This information is used only for abuse prevention, and will not be associated with your identity.

When do we collect information?

No information is collected by Orígenes.

How do we use your information?

No information is collected by Orígenes.

How do we protect your information?

No information is collected by Orígenes.

Third-party disclosure

Orígenes does not sell, trade or otherwise transfer your information.

Third-party products or services

Orígenes does not include or offer third-party products or services.
