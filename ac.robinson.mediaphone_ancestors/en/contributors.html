Orígenes contributors and licenses

Orígenes is based on the open source project Com-Phone Story Maker, which itself builds on the work of many contributors and open source projects. For more information about Com-Phone Story Maker, please see the project's homepage, and its list of contributors and open source software.

Orígenes itself is licensed under the LGPL version 3.

Design and development

As well as building on the work of other open source projects, Orígenes was developed as part of a co-design process between indigenous peoples in Southern Patagonia and a research team linking computer science, humanities and social sciences. The work was funded by the CHERISH Digital Economy Research Centre. For more information about the Orígenes project, please see the project homepage.
