Com-Phone Story Maker privacy policy

This privacy policy has been compiled to better serve those who are concerned with how their Personally Identifiable Information is being used. Personally Identifiable Information is information that can be used on its own or with other information to identify, contact, or locate a single person, or to identify an individual in context. Please read our privacy policy carefully to get a clear understanding of how we collect, use, protect or otherwise handle your Personally Identifiable Information.

What personal information do we collect?

Com-Phone Story Maker does not collect or share any personal information.

Any information you choose to capture using Com-Phone Story Maker (e.g., photos, audio recordings, text) is stored only on your own device. Com-Phone Story Maker does not have permission to access the internet, so your stories and media cannot be transmitted unless you choose to do so using another app or service, at which point other privacy policies may apply.

When do we collect information?

No information is collected by Com-Phone Story Maker.

How do we use your information?

No information is collected by Com-Phone Story Maker.

How do we protect your information?

No information is collected by Com-Phone Story Maker.

Third-party disclosure

Com-Phone Story Maker does not sell, trade or otherwise transfer your information.

Third-party products or services

Com-Phone Story Maker does not include or offer third-party products or services.
